# Pricing Tiers

These are draft offer tiers for paid support around the free Mac Migration Operator Skill. Do not publish prices, accept payment, or promise availability until the maintainer approves the final offer.

## Positioning

The free Skill is the trust builder. Paid offers sell confidence, guidance, validation, and recovery support for people who do not want to manage a high-stakes Mac migration alone.

## Recommended Tiers

| Tier | Draft price | Best for | Includes | Delivery |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- | --- |
| Self-Serve | Free | Users comfortable running their own migration | Open Skill, docs, templates, examples, read-only helper scripts | GitHub and Skill directories |
| Migration Plan Review | $99 | Users before Migration Assistant or a manual transfer | Review intake, risks, transfer method, source-freeze checklist, and rollback plan | Async review or 30-minute call |
| Guided Migration Validation | $249 | Users who already migrated and want confidence before unplugging the old Mac | New-Mac validation checklist, app/account checks, project-folder checks, cloud/external drive review, gap list | Async plus 60-minute call |
| Live Migration Operator | $499 | Users doing the move in one focused session | Pre-flight plan, Migration Assistant guidance, first-boot validation, missing-file triage, retirement gate | 2-hour live session plus written checklist |
| Small Business Cutover | $1,250 | Founders, consultants, creators, and small teams with active projects | Multi-project inventory, cloud-drive and external-drive review, developer-tool checks, automation review, post-migration smoke tests, final handoff packet | Half-day remote support |
| Team or White-Glove | Custom | Businesses with multiple Macs, production access, or regulated data | Scoped plan, migration runbook, validation packet, stakeholder checklist, rollback plan | Quote after intake |

## Pricing Logic

The paid offer should charge for risk reduction, not for the files. The highest value is in preventing lost work, avoiding broad overwrites, catching account and cloud-sync gaps early, and giving the user a clear decision point before the old Mac is retired.

## Guarantee Draft

Use a careful guarantee that avoids promising technical outcomes outside our control:

```text
If the session does not produce a clear migration plan, validation checklist, or prioritized gap list, I will either continue async until those are delivered or refund the service fee.
```

Do not guarantee that every missing file can be recovered, every account can be restored, or every third-party app will transfer correctly.

## Approval Gates

Maintainer approval is required before:

- Publishing prices outside the repository.
- Creating checkout links.
- Connecting Stripe, PayPal, Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy, Capafy payouts, or any other payment account.
- Taking a paid customer.
- Promising live availability or response times.

Next step: choose the first public offer. The safest launch offer is `Migration Plan Review` or `Guided Migration Validation` because it avoids live transfer risk while proving demand.
